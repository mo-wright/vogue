About the Project

This project analyzes historic issues of Vogue Magazine, sourced from their historical archives.

We focused on the section “What She Wears.” Our XML markup tracks how fashion details evolved from the 1890s to 1995.

About the Team

Our team includes Tara, Molly, Trinity, and Miracle. We analyzed descriptions, illustrations, fabrics, colors, and garments.

Background

Vogue began in 1892 as a publication documenting New York society. Over time, its fashion reporting reflected major cultural shifts.

Natural fibers dominated early issues, while the mid-century introduced synthetics, new shapes, and evolving fashion language.

Main Goal

We aim to show how Vogue shaped and reflected beauty standards over a century of reporting.
